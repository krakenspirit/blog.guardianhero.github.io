Japanese Bed

Sep 7, 2018

I made this camping bed out of an old japanese roll-up futon, a deflated lightspeed bed, an eggshell foam matt inserted into the lightspeed bed (for waterproofing), and that’s it. The Sleeping bag serves as a really warm blanket that has been with me since 2014. My only problem with this bed is that if it’s placed directly on the floor of the camp or children pee on it it won’t be easily washable. So my next goal is to find a low-cost solution to wrap it up. My other challenge is that it’s not very great for backpacking in places where you can’t take your car – so it’s great for car camping, but not for outdoors.

I think if I can store it well enough, this and my teton cot brought back in 2014 might actually be a good combo, so long as it doesn't take too much space in the prius.
