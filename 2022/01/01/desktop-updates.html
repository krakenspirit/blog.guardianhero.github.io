Hardware Updates

Jan 1, 2022

Built my own PC to replace my old laptop. Invested in a PC build designed to work off-grid in a small space, but also capable of doing the work I need done. Now I can test games, work and experiment with open-sourced A.I, and can 3D Model in Blender using a better GPU than my old acer laptop. I still use my Laptop for writing and testing older games, but the bulk of my work is on my PC.

My current build:

  • EVGA RTX 3060
  • Asus Rog Strix B550-I
  • Ryzen 7 5700G
  • Corsair Vengeance RGB 32GB (2x16GB)
  • Corsair iCue Sp120 RGM 120mm Fan, single pack
  • Crucial P2 500GB NvMe PCie M.2 SSD - for “OS only”
  • Samsung 980 1TB SSD
  • Corsair SF Series, SF750, 750 Watts, 80+ Gold Certified
  • Cooler Master NR200 - Mini-ITX case
  • Monitor is old TCL - Roku TV

Other tools purchased for the projects: Soldering Station, 18TB Seagate, 14TB Seagate, 200w Renogy solar panels w/solar connectors and adapters, and garden seeds.
