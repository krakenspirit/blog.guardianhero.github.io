About

Guardian & Hero Nation

Many monastics are forced to stop practicing or place a hold on their spiritual journey while they care for children and seniors. They may struggle to make ends meet, and may require mental health or medical services that are outside the scope of their financial capabilities. When forced to choose between doing work aligned with their spirit and beliefs, and their family's care, they often have little choice but to choose to perform services and work that are not in alignment with their spirit or the philosophies of the nations, just to make ends meet. As a result of this dilemma we worked to provide a solution.

Our Mission is to create art, products and services aligned with our spiritual beliefs, each as a byproduct of our healing, build self-sufficient monasteries inspired by pastoral nomads and traditional rural farming communities, and to design and develop holistic environments for our seniors, children and vulnerable members in need of care. We do so by combining our resources and skills to build self-contained communities which provide services to each other, and through products and/or services provided to the community in alignment with our spiritual beliefs, philosophy and goals.
