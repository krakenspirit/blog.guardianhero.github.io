Credits

Unsplash Images: All images that were not taken by me came from "Unsplash". With the exception of some stock photos that came with a premium multi-purpose theme called "TheGem". All image files for Unsplash have the name of the creator and the word 'unsplash'.

Personal Images: Most personal images and photos taken by me will have a Guardian + Hero Nation watermark, all will be marked by _GHN in the filename. GHN photos are not meant to be used outside of this site.

Horse Rescue: Some photos were taken by volunteers I worked beside at a horse rescue I volunteered at for several years. I tried my best to remember who took those photos and have placed their name and the approximate dates they were taken in the filename. I also individually watermarked each of those photos as HPS - Horse Protection Society, the place where they were taken. The woman who took most of those photos started her own horse rescue called Midnight's Promise a few years back named after Midnight one of the horses at the HPS. These photos are not meant to be taken off this site, but if they are at the very least the horse rescue will receive some word of mouth.

Theme: The current active theme uses code from Fresh and Journal. I tinkered with the source codes and used it to broaden my understanding of how Jekyll works. Of all the themes I found the Journal theme was my favourite. I hope to purchase the official Journal theme by the end of summer as their premium themes are up to date with 1:1 support and some pretty sweet features that I've yet to emulate myself.


Blog Format

Stock Photos: With few exceptions, I utilize attractive stock photos mostly taken from unsplash as the intro photo.

Collage: Because I don't use a CMS ("Content Management System") for this blog, every photo has to be uploaded and put in by hand. I don't have wordpress to lean on for resizing and uploading my photos automatically for me. I decided it would be easier to just collage multiple photos. Maybe that might change in the future, but for right now, this entire site is mostly manual.

No Sitemap: I have the option of adding a sitemap, but have decided not to. As interesting as the past is, this blog is about the present.


Privacy & Updates

I believe that privacy is equal to safety. For this reason I only use my government name when dealing with government authorities. Online and in the world I use an alias for myself and my children. I tell select people my government name and the exact location of where I am going through text or email, and am in the process of developing an emergency protocol in case something happens on my journey. I post updates of what I’ve done only after it has been done, or after I’ve already been there. I choose not to post publicly online where I am now, in my exact location. If I do post online where I am now in my exact location, I will not post the address, the name of the name of the farm I am working with, or any details that could give away the current address until after I am gone.

The only time I am open to naming a person or a place is if (1) that person or place has a website listing their name and (2) if I no longer have regular contact with that person or place.
