Martial Arts

Feb 1, 2016

6 Months after giving birth to Little Fig, I joined Shaolin & Tai Chi. I often say that little fig gave me life, but this school and the mentor who took me under his wing really helped shaped me. Underneath their guidance and compassion I really grew and blossomed and had learned the foundations for self-discipline. I can only hope that the skills I learned from my time here will not only travel with me, but serve as a foundation for what will be the rest of my life.

My life and interests are and continue to be heavily influenced by XWP, Zoe Bell, the Last Airbender/Legend of Korra and Shaolin Monastery documentaries. I’ve always been determined to discover myself, grow and make dreams happen, and my birthday gift to me was to take that a step further. It came down to two choices. Reach out to a spiritual therapist or a Shaman (I had my heart set on Hudson Leick), or join a martial arts school focused on mind body spirit growth and integration. I could only afford one or the other, so I figured I’d give the school a try and here I am, there I was. My vision evolved and I went from wanting to live in a cave and a tent to – wanting to buy land and live in my monastery. Now my Raison d’etre is to acquire the skills I need to take back to my Guardian monastery.
