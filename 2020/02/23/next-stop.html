Start Here!

Feb 23, 2020

The hardest decision I made this year was to leave my Kung Fu Family and go on a new adventure without any guarantee what the future might bring. This year, I am traveling the country to study Parkour, camp and volunteer exchange. The hope is to acquire the skills I need to build my monastery, expand my family and gain confidence with my ability to network and generate income as a web developer, A.I Programmer and writer.

In 2020 we'll spend some time traveling the states, and then a few months in Canada. If little fig gets excepted into Spanish Language Immersion school, there will be slight modifications but no changes. After 2020 if all goes well we plan to travel abroad throughout Europe and possibly South America for 3 years. Our hope between 2021 and 2023 is to purchase land in the U.S and at the end of the 3 years purchase land abroad where we hope to live 6 Months working to acquire residency, and 3-6 Months in the USA.


March Update:

March Update: At the end of February I was assaulted in a road rage incident. An old black Toyota Corolla without a license plate perceived I cut them off and deliberately rammed into my Prius. Fortunately no one but me was in the vehicle and we were covered by good insurance. The car went into the shop at the start of March and will be out at the end of the week. I am continuing to prepare for our original journey within the USA and abroad in Canada, but there's been a slight modification.

The thing that bothered me the most, wasn't that the vehicle hit me. It was that the woman hit me and wasn't held accountable for her actions. It bothers me that in our society we can hurt other people and for the most part if you don't know your assailant the chances of them getting away with it is pretty high. The officer I spoke to recommended I get a gun to protect me and my child, and I thought about it. And while I will never carry a gun in my house after weighing all possible scenarios I could think of, I did decide that I will receive tactical training and want to be someone who helps solve crimes and bring people who hurt others to justice. For 72 hours I played with the idea of becoming a volunteer police officer, EMT, and 911 Dispatcher. After the first 48 hours I realized that I am unable to dedicate the fulltime required to be a 911 Dispatcher and that I am not compatible for being an American Police Officer. Though there is still opportunity for me to pursue Volunteer EMT.

My hope is now that I can find a country that aligns with my beliefs and become an Officer and 911 Dispatcher in that country. And then to use that experience to volunteer at places like O.U.R. Rescue and/or similar, bringing those skills and experiences back to my own Guardian Monastery. Should I fail to do even that my hope falls on utilizing the Guardian Monastery to fill a void in providing at a minimum aftercare. There's a special place in my heart for pedophiles, rapists, and people who torture and hurt others. And more than anything I want to be part of the solution in making the world a better place, not just in the way I live but in this world where it feels like the bad people are winning.
